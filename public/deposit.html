Premium % - calculated each month, will be added to the sum only at the end of the deposit. Leave 0 if you don't have it.

Calculations example:
3 months, initial 10 000, under 12%, premium 1%, month add 1000.

End of Month 1:
a) calculate premium percents: 10 000 * 0.01 / 12 = 8.33
b) calculate percents: 10 000 * 0.12 / 12 = 100
c) add percents to the sum: 10 000 + 100 = 10 100
d) manual added money: 10 100 + 1000 = 11 100

End of Month 2:
a) calculate premium percents: 11 100 * 0.01 / 12 = 9.25
b) sum premium percents 8.33 + 9.25 = 17.58
c) calculate percents: 11 100 * 0.12 / 12 = 111
d) add percents to the sum: 11 100 + 111 = 11 211
e) manual added money: 11 211 + 1000 = 12 211

End of Month 3:
a) calculate premium percents: 12 211 * 0.01 / 12 = 10.18
b) sum premium percents 17.58 + 10.18 = 27.76
c) calculate percents: 11 211 * 0.12 / 12 = 122.11
d) add percents to the sum: 12 211 + 122.11 = 12 333.11
e) add manual added money: 12 333.11 + 1000 = 13 333.11
f) manual added money: 13 333.11 + 27.76 = 13 360.76

Result:
End sum: 13 361, percents: 333.11, premium percents: 27.76, manually added: 3 000. Month income: M1 100, M2 111, M3 122.11
